SAT Prep Resources

Qualifications

I scored a 1580 on the SAT on my first attempt, with a perfect score (800) in math and 780 in English. I didn't spend hours studying vocabulary or reviewing formulas; I just had a good strategy for taking the test and had clear principles going into it, which enabled me to answer the questions quickly, correctly, and without stress. Learn and practice the same methods in this course.

Registration Information

What's in a Class?

Here is some information on the class structure. This will be flexible, and I'll keep adjusting it to best suit my students, but tentatively, regular small-group online classes will include:

  • Lecture time, covering key concepts to know for the test
  • Strategy discussion, going over best practices for the test
  • Instructed problem solving time, demonstrations of a few sample questions being solved, making use of good test-taking and problem-solving strategies
  • Group activites, like Kahoots to encourage friendly competition and help with speed in question-solving
  • Homework/resource descriptions, assigning resources to review on Google Classroom/Canvas and providing additional practice problems (or assessments, every once in a while) to check for understanding

Office hours will be Q&A sessions and times for more personalized coaching, although they will be limited. More specific, individual questions can be asked on Google Chat, where I respond quickly. The Canvas/Google Classroom resource will have lecture content, like slides, vocabulary lists, etc. and will also host quizzes and home practice material.
